COMMENTS TUTORIAL BACKGROUND

Lazarus

A single father with his only daughter. Lazarus loved his daughter Isabella to the moon and back, she was the only light he ever needed in his life. A middle-class worker who's continuously working on the construction site to put food on the table; A hardworking father whose only wish is to put a smile on his only child. But the world had other plans, a couple of months before the outbreak Isabella was diagnosed with acute leukemia. When the outbreak finally spread, he arrived at the hospital to quickly get Isabella and was trying to head over to the nearest rescue camp when he bumped into the road with 2 girls trying to run away from the feasters. He quickly helped them and for a while they traveled to find the camp, Isabella was getting worse… One bad night they were hunting for a place to stay when a nearby oil factory went up in flames and attracted the feasters. Isabella snatched one of the fast feasters. And was eaten right in front of everyone. Including Lazarus.

Onyx

A queer child who constantly looked for acknowledgement from others. Onyx grew up in the rescue camp surrounded by people who always treated him like a slave. He never met his parents and never had someone who showed him what a role model should be. He lingered continuously in the camp and upon reaching a certain age was assigned to help in the kitchen but was continuously bullied by one of the main families in the camp which made him into a stubborn person who always only thought about himself.

Abaddon

Before the outbreak, Abaddon was a sophomore in his community college. Eager to continuously rise above everyone else he became entitled to his work. When the outbreak hit he found a camp and stayed there for a while. But when the camp was run down by one feaster he saw a glimpse of his glory and quickly killed the feaster after the reckless massacre it caused. Since then he became the leader of the camp but never acknowledged the people who were working beside him. All he did was order everyone to what he thought was right, a hot headed tempered man who proposed himself as a good guy.

Lilith

Before the outbreak happened, Lilith was just a freshman at her university. A simple girl with a perfect family who supported everything she wanted to achieve and be in life; A person full of love and hope. But when the global outbreak hit, she witnessed how her parents turned into feasters which almost killed her. But then she met River who saved her from being bitten and turning into one of them and since then she found her purpose to continuously move forward.

Luci

Coming from a family of immigrants, she always thrived to survive. Once was a criminal being hunted by the police before the outbreak happened but when it all broke loose. She fought many feasters before finding the camp and was assigned as the main guard of the facility. But she’s a maniac who always threatens people with her authority. She is very short tempered and easily hurt the person that triggers her rage.

River

Growing up in an abusive home, River’s mindset in things is different. A reckless rebel child that dropped out of school to pursue their passion in music. Eventually, she independently got herself out of that house and frequently played music at many cafes and bars around their area. But she always had her eyes on this one girl who was a regular at the cafe where she usually does her gigs. She never knew her name. But when the outbreak happened, she was able to cross paths with her in the most horrible way ever. Being chased by feasters was it. How romantic.

Feasters

A monster that came out of wheat products. Before the outbreak the researchers experimented with different chemicals trying to create a parasite that rapidly helps plants grow. Nothing went wrong during the experimentation, but when the foods were processed it made people sick which quickly turned them into feasters, one bite from any of the ones who turned can turn you into one of them in the span 2 days.
