Info


This game is based on “Resident Evil” and “The Last of Us”. An apocalyptic game mainly to entertain people who love horror and gore. This game serves as a way to remind the audience to be grateful for everything they have right now. To continuously thrive while also helping others to grow with them. This game tackles many issues regarding family, personal, and environment that may serve to reflect on life.

Summary


An outbreak happened during 2021 which spread throughout different nations of the world. A group of friends were made in between the commotion, all of them lost someone during the outbreak this made them treat each other like family. The government broadcasted a nearby rescue camp and this began their journey to survive the ongoing pandemic the government caused.
